Who We Are

To empower every Ethiopian with affordable, accessible, and transparent grocery shopping through direct sourcing and digital innovation. We envision a future where every household in Ethiopia-regardless of income -has access to reliable, affordable groceries delivered through smart technology. GulitNet aims to be the trusted bridge between farmers and families across the nation.
